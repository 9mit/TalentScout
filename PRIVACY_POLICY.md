# Privacy Policy - TalentScout Career Suite

**Last Updated:** December 2025

## 1. Introduction

TalentScout Career Suite ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in compliance with GDPR and other data privacy regulations.

## 2. Data Controller

**Application Name:** TalentScout Career Suite  
**Components:** TalentScout (Recruiter Mode) & PrepMaster (Candidate Mode)  
**Data Storage:** Local SQLite database (career_suite.db)

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 PrepMaster (Candidate Mode)
- Quiz performance data (scores, timestamps)
- Selected programming languages and difficulty levels
- Quiz questions and answers (anonymized)
- Performance analytics (aggregated)

### 3.2 TalentScout (Recruiter Mode)
- Candidate names and email addresses
- Position and tech stack information
- Interview conversation data
- Screening results

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
- **Consent:** You explicitly consent to data collection when using the application
- **Legitimate Interest:** Improving our services and providing analytics
- **Contract Performance:** Delivering the quiz and screening services you request

## 5. Data Usage

Your data is used to:
- Provide quiz questions and evaluate performance
- Generate performance analytics and insights
- Store quiz history for progress tracking
- Improve AI-generated content quality

## 6. Data Storage and Security

### 6.1 Local Storage
- All data is stored locally in SQLite database (career_suite.db)
- No data is transmitted to external servers (except AI API calls)
- Database file is stored on your local machine

### 6.2 Security Measures
- Database access restricted to application only
- API keys stored in .env file (not in code)
- No plain-text storage of sensitive information
- Regular security updates recommended

## 7. Data Retention

- **Quiz Data:** Retained indefinitely unless manually deleted
- **Candidate Data:** Retained until manually deleted
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

### 8.1 Right to Access
Request a copy of all your stored data

### 8.2 Right to Rectification
Correct any inaccurate personal data

### 8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
Request deletion of your personal data

### 8.4 Right to Data Portability
Export your data in a machine-readable format

### 8.5 Right to Restrict Processing
Limit how we use your data

### 8.6 Right to Object
Object to data processing based on legitimate interests

### 8.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time

## 9. Third-Party Services

### 9.1 Google Gemini AI
- Used for generating quiz questions and analyzing answers
- Data sent: Question prompts, user answers (anonymized)
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- No personal identifiers sent to AI service

## 10. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:
- Third-party advertisers
- Data brokers
- Marketing companies
- Any external parties (except Google Gemini for AI processing)

## 11. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 12. International Data Transfers

Data is stored locally on your machine. AI API calls to Google Gemini may involve international data transfers subject to Google's privacy policies.

## 13. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify affected users within 72 hours
- Report to relevant supervisory authorities
- Take immediate action to secure the breach

## 14. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

## 15. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the application.

## 16. Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights:

- Use the built-in data export/delete features

## 17. Compliance Certifications

This application is designed to comply with:

- Other applicable data privacy laws
